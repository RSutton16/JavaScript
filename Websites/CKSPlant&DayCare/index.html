Welcome to Cora Kay Daycare!

Cora Kay Daycare is operated in Canton, Missouri. I offer a safe and nurturing environment for children ages 6 weeks to 12 years. Cora is dedicated to providing a fun and educational experience for your child. I offer a variety of activities including arts and crafts, outdoor play, and story time. I also provide healthy meals and snacks for your child. Our goal is to help your child grow and develop in a positive way. We look forward to welcoming your child to our daycare!
