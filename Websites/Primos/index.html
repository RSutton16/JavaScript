Welcome To Primo's!

Everyone's favorite place to dine in or carry-out a menu that includes: pizza, burgers, subs and wraps, hand dipped ice cream and traditional soda fountain favorites! There’s nothing better than the smell of pizza fresh from the oven. When we opened our shop here in Canton, Missouri back in 1997, we dreamed of sharing our love for homemade pizza with our community. For two decades, Primo’s has been a downtown landmark, serving our famous signature pies like the MoMo Monster and the TrueMan. But the menu doesn’t stop there: We also offer subs, burgers, fries, and even 19 different flavors of ice cream. With our loyalty program offering points on purchases that add up to store credit, we hope you’ll make us your go-to restaurant when you’re craving a fun and casual night out.
